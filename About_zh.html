我透過設計拉近人與科技之間的距離

我是芊綺 (Chelsea)，現在是台大資管所的學生。擁有網頁開發與行為研究的背景，因此在設計時能夠考量技術可行性並從多元的觀點思考。

我曾經在營建領域的產學合作專案中擔任 UI/UX 設計實習生，讓我更確信自己未來想要往設計師的職涯發展。為了累積更多 UX 設計與研究經驗，我透過參與活動與修習課程積極尋找累積專案的機會。

在空閒時間，我沉浸於各式各樣的戶外運動，像是健行、騎腳踏車、衝浪以及游泳。除此之外，我也喜歡旅遊。我特別享受在旅途中嘗試新體驗、與來自不同文化的人互動、 並透過 vlog 與攝影紀錄我的旅程。

I design to bring technology closer to people.

I am Chien-Chi (Chelsea), a UX designer and developer from Taiwan.

I'm currently pursuing a graduate degree in Information Management at National Taiwan University. With a background in web development, data science, and business, I always consider technical feasibility during design and think from a multidisciplinary perspective.

Previously, I worked as a UI/UX Design Intern in a cooperation project, which fueled my passion to continue my career as a designer. To gain more experience in UX research and design, I actively seek opportunities through volunteer and school projects. With my strong leadership and communication skills, I'm also proud to lead the research and design of the PDOGs Redesign Project and serve as a technical teaching assistant at this year's OpenHCI Workshop.

In my free time, I immerse myself in outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, surfing, and swimming. I'm also an avid traveler who likes to explore new experiences, interact with people around the world, and document my journey through photography and vlogs.
